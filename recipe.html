PANIR MASALA

Ingredient

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
